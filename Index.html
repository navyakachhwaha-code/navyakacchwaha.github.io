Our Story

A studio built on the belief that every memory deserves a masterpiece.

Picture this: Nashik's golden afternoon light flooding through a studio window, falling on a newborn fast asleep in a parent's trembling hands. That is what we do every single day at Takshvi Photo Studio — we freeze the light. Nestled in Balwant Nagar, Anandvalli, we opened our doors in 2023 with one conviction: that the stories written in your eyes deserve to outlive you.

From the hush of a maternity session to the exuberant blur of a wedding morning, our team approaches every frame as though it is the last photograph ever taken — because for you, it might be the one you carry forever.
